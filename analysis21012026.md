## Detailed Analysis

### 1. Halving Cycle Analysis

We are now 641 days post-halving, which places us firmly in the historically bullish mid-to-late cycle phase. Previous cycles peaked at:
- **2012 Cycle:** ~370 days post-halving
- **2016 Cycle:** ~518 days post-halving
- **2020 Cycle:** ~546 days post-halving

The current cycle is running extended compared to historical patterns, which we attribute to:
1. **Institutional adoption** - ETFs create sustained demand but also more sophisticated trading
2. **Macro sensitivity** - Bitcoin now responds to Fed policy and global liquidity
3. **Market maturity** - Reduced volatility, longer consolidation phases

At 641 days, we are approximately 95-123 days past where previous cycles peaked. However, the October 2025 high of $126,272 may not have been THE cycle top given:
- MVRV never reached euphoric levels (stayed well below 7)
- NUPL never entered "Euphoria" zone
- No retail frenzy or mainstream media peak attention

**Assessment:** Current pullback is a mid-cycle correction, not the end of the bull market.
